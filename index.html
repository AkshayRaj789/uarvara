About

Urvara Farm in Kanthaloor is a renowned organic farm nestled in the picturesque Western Ghats of Kerala, India. The farm is celebrated for its commitment to sustainable farming practices and its lush biodiversity

Urvara Farm in Kanthaloor stands as a shining example of how sustainable farming practices can not only yield high-quality produce but also contribute to the well-being of the environment and local communities.

Location

Situated in the idyllic village of Kanthaloor, Urvara Farm benefits from the region's favorable climate and rich soil, ideal for cultivating a variety of crops.

Organic Farming

Urvara Farm prides itself on its organic farming methods, eschewing chemical fertilizers and pesticides in favor of natural alternatives. This approach not only ensures the production of healthy, chemical-free crops but also helps preserve the local ecosystem.

Biodiversity

The farm is a haven for biodiversity, with a diverse range of flora and fauna thriving within its boundaries. This biodiversity not only contributes to the ecological balance but also enhances the beauty of the surroundings.

Crops

Urvara Farm cultivates a wide range of crops, including fruits, vegetables, spices, and medicinal plants. Some of the notable crops grown here include strawberries, passion fruit, avocado, pepper, cardamom, and vanilla.

Agro-Tourism

In addition to its farming activities, Urvara Farm also promotes agro-tourism, allowing visitors to experience the beauty of rural life and learn about sustainable agriculture firsthand.

Community Engagement

The farm actively engages with the local community, providing employment opportunities and supporting initiatives aimed at rural development and environmental conservation.
